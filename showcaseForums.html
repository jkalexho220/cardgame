We are proud to announce a new map that we have been collaborating on for close to two years! HeavenGames CCG is a collectible card game where you build a deck of units and spells and battle foes. There are over 150 cards, with endless combinations to make! This map features a singleplayer deck-building and story mode, as well as multiplayer duels between friends!

Now it's time for the fun part: The cards! We will be revealing new cards each week until release, so stay tuned! Here's how to read a card in the showcase:

  • Cost: A card will cost mana to play. You acquire an increasing amount of mana each turn.
  • Health: When a unit’s health reaches zero, it dies!
  • Attack: This is how much damage a unit deals with its attack.
  • Speed: This is how many spaces a unit can move per turn.
  • Range: This is how many spaces away a unit can attack.
  • Keywords: Shown in bold, keywords grant special abilities to units.
  • Effect: Some cards have additional special effects, which will be detailed below.
Each card belongs to one of five different classes. When building your deck, you can choose cards from two different classes. In addition, you must choose a Commander from one of the two classes to lead your army!

Our first class is the Adventurer class. Lometown is a popular spot for adventurers. Here resides one of the largest branches of the Adventurer’s Guild, and the city is in good relations with the neighboring Elves. Recently, the Wandering Troupe led by phdorogers4 has dropped by to grace the city with festivities and music.

Commander: phdorogers4

The first Commander of the game, and the protagonist of our story, phdorogers4 is a solid commander who can refresh the actions of allies with the 'Windsong' spell. You can even cast this on himself to chain multiple attacks in one turn!

Keyword: Guard

Adventurers make sure to always watch each others’ backs with the Guard keyword. Units with this keyword will take an attack for an adjacent ally by swapping places with them before the attack hits.

Keyword: Pathfinder

Adventurers have the tools to get anywhere. Units with the Pathfinder keyword can pass through other units and traverse difficult terrain.
