We are proud to announce a new map that we have been collaborating on for close to two years! HeavenGames CCG is a collectible card game where you build a deck of units and spells and battle foes. There are over 150 cards, with endless combinations to make! This map features a singleplayer deck-building and story mode told with comics, as well as multiplayer duels between friends!

Mix and Match

When building your deck, you can add cards from up to two different classes to it. Unlikely combos may be revealed!

Choose your Commander

Choose a Commander to lead your army! You only have one, and if they die, you lose! Each Commander brings a different game plan to battle. Use their unique strengths to your advantage!

Our first class is the Adventurer class. Lometown is a popular spot for adventurers. Here resides one of the largest branches of the Adventurer’s Guild, and the city is in good relations with the neighboring Elves. Recently, the Wandering Troupe led by phdorogers4 has dropped by to grace the city with festivities and music.

Commander: phdorogers4

The first Commander of the game, and the protagonist of our story, phdorogers4 is a solid commander who can refresh the actions of allies with the 'Windsong' spell. You can even cast this on himself to chain multiple attacks in one turn!

Keyword: Guard

Adventurers make sure to always watch each others’ backs with the Guard keyword. Units with this keyword will take an attack for an adjacent ally by swapping places with them before the attack hits.

Keyword: Pathfinder

Adventurers have the tools to get anywhere. Units with the Pathfinder keyword can pass through other units and traverse difficult terrain.

Royal Scenario Academy is the most prestigious school of magic in the land, drawing students from all walks of life. Nobles and magic apprentices alike gather at this school to learn the secrets that shape the world. Graduates of the Academy generally have two career paths: They can join the Research Tower, where horrifying and inhumane research is conducted by the mad scientist nottud, or they can join the Royal Magic Division.

Commander: nanodude

The headmaster of the academy with questionable credentials, nanodude discounts the spells in your hand, allowing you to pull off some nasty combos.

Spells, Spells, Spells

While lacking in physical prowess, students of the Arcane utilize a variety of spells to battle their foes. This class has the highest number of spells of any class, but their units are on the weaker side.

Keyword: Healer

Learning magic is a difficult task, and it sometimes comes with dangerous accidents. When a student suffers an injury, the teachers are there to reprimand and heal them with the Healer keyword. Units with this keyword cannot attack or counterattack. Instead, they can use their action to heal an ally.

Led by scragins, an exiled group of people found a new home at the bottom of the ocean. These people created the flourishing metropolis known as the Undercity. However, all was not peaceful, for they had misplaced an angry coven of sea witches known as the Naga, who were determined to get their oceans back. Furthermore, one of the Undercity people by the name of Out Reach had forsaken his humanity, becoming a sorcerer that controlled the ancient, massive creatures of the Deep.

Commander: Out Reach

The eel sorcerer Out Reach allows you to accumulate Manaflow even when casting spells so you can save it for summoning the powerful monsters of the deep.

Mana Manipulation

Water can take any shape, but sometimes it overflows. Your Manaflow is equal to your unspent mana from your previous turn. Various cards can take advantage of this resource.

Power of the Ocean

The ocean currents are powerful forces. When a unit is Pushed, it will be launched in a straight line until it hits another unit or difficult terrain. If a unit is Pushed into another one, they will attack each other.
